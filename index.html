Professional Bio

I am a creative media student at Texas Tech University interested in web design, animation, visual effects, and film-centered storytelling. My work combines technical front-end skills with a strong interest in how images, motion, layout, and atmosphere shape the way people experience a story. I enjoy designing websites that feel polished, cinematic, and easy to navigate.

Through my coursework, I have built projects that explore branding, layout systems, responsive design, media presentation, and visual storytelling. I am especially drawn to creative work that connects entertainment and design, whether that means building a streaming-style website, presenting a film production concept, or designing a portfolio that feels memorable. My goal is to keep developing as a designer and storyteller while creating work that could support future opportunities in animation, VFX, web design, or creative media.

Case Study

The Dentist’s Aquarium

Overview: The Dentist’s Aquarium is an interactive narrative project published on Itch.io, designed to immerse users in a story-driven experience.

Challenge: The main challenge was creating an engaging narrative that keeps users interested while navigating through the experience.

My Role: I developed the concept, structured the story, and designed how users interact with the experience.

Process: I focused on pacing, story structure, and visual presentation to guide users through the narrative in a clear and engaging way.

Solution: The final project presents an interactive storytelling experience where users progress through the narrative using visual and structural cues.

Results: The project demonstrates my ability to combine storytelling with digital platforms and user interaction.

Reflection: I learned that pacing, clarity, and structure are key to creating engaging interactive stories.

Case Study

Gallery

Overview: Gallery is a web-based project designed to showcase images in a clean, organized layout with a focus on visual presentation and user experience.

Challenge: The challenge was designing a layout that highlights visual content while keeping navigation simple and intuitive.

My Role: I built the layout, structured the HTML, styled the interface with CSS, and focused on spacing and responsiveness.

Process: I experimented with grid-based layouts, image sizing, and spacing to create a balanced and visually appealing gallery.

Solution: The final design uses structured layout sections, consistent spacing, and responsive scaling to display images effectively.

Results: The project demonstrates my ability to build clean layouts and present visual content in a user-friendly way.

Reflection: I learned how important spacing, alignment, and responsiveness are when designing content-heavy pages.
